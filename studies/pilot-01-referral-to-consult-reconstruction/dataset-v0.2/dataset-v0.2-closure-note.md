# Dataset v0.2 Closure Note

Dataset v0.2.8 is closed after the limited coding pass.

## Why v0.2.8 Was Closed

Dataset v0.2.8 completed the bounded workflow it was authorized to test:

- limited educational abstraction for `ABS-003` and `ABS-004`;
- limited reconstruction coding for `ABS-003` and `ABS-004`;
- human post-coding review of the limited coding outputs.

The human review decision accepted the limited coding outputs with caution and closed v0.2.8 after this pass. The decision did not authorize additional coding, abstraction, or expansion.

## Why Remaining Candidates Remain On Hold

The remaining candidates remain on hold because v0.2.8 was a controlled two-case step, not an authorization to open the full Dataset v0.2 candidate batch.

Opening additional candidates would require a new gate decision, renewed privacy and copyright review, and explicit authorization.

## Why Research Note 002 Remains Prohibited

Research Note 002 remains prohibited because the current v0.2 work is still a bounded methodological dry run.

The available outputs are not sufficient to support a public research note, empirical claim, frequency estimate, clinical validity claim, or generalizable finding.

## Why Full Dataset v0.2 Expansion Is Not Authorized

Full Dataset v0.2 expansion is not authorized because:

- the current evidence comes from limited educational abstractions only;
- the coding outputs were accepted with caution;
- remaining methodological questions have not been resolved;
- no second-coder reliability workflow has been completed for v0.2.8;
- no gate has authorized broader abstraction or coding.

## Requirements Before Future Expansion

Before any future expansion, the project would need:

- a new explicit gate decision;
- review of whether v0.2.8 abstraction depth was sufficient;
- refinement of compression versus uncertainty-loss coding rules;
- a second-coder or adjudication plan;
- renewed source-distance, privacy, and copyright checks;
- explicit confirmation that future work remains educational, methodology-focused, non-clinical-guidance, and non-validation.

Until those conditions are met, Dataset v0.2 remains closed at the v0.2.8 stopping point.
