# Privacy and Identifiability Checklist

Use this checklist to determine whether the abstraction includes patient-identifying or source-recognizable details.

Reviewer decision options:

- low risk
- medium risk
- high risk
- revise before coding

## ABS-001

Check for:

- [ ] names
- [ ] initials
- [ ] exact age
- [ ] exact dates
- [ ] institution names
- [ ] geographic identifiers
- [ ] rare demographic combinations
- [ ] record numbers
- [ ] unusually specific temporal sequence
- [ ] rare-case details not needed for cognitive-transition testing
- [ ] details that could make the source case too recognizable
