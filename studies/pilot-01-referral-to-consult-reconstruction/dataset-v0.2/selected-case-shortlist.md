# Selected Case Shortlist

Shortlist of 10 candidates for Dataset v0.2 abstraction. This file records source metadata and selection rationale only. No case text has been copied.

## Selection Criteria

Selected candidates were prioritized for:

- clear initial presentation or referral-like artifact;
- clear downstream assessment, specialist interpretation, or final plan;
- enough cognitive transition to support reconstruction;
- low or manageable privacy risk;
- manageable copyright risk through citation-only metadata and paraphrase;
- specialty diversity;
- usefulness for testing compression versus uncertainty-loss coding.
