## Referral Letter

### Referral Reason

State the reason for referral or consultation.

### Suspected Diagnosis

State the referring clinician's working diagnosis, suspected diagnosis, or differential.

### Uncertainty Statements

List explicit uncertainty, ambiguity, caveats, unresolved questions, or probability language.

### Requested Consultation

State the specific specialist question, opinion, evaluation, procedure, or decision requested.

### Contextual Details

Include relevant history, findings, patient constraints, prior treatment, imaging, labs, or social context.

### Timeline

Describe the sequence of symptoms, findings, prior care, referral timing, and urgency.

## Specialist Consultation Note

### Final Assessment

State the specialist's final assessment or primary clinical interpretation.

### Differential Diagnosis

List alternative diagnoses, competing explanations, or ruled-out possibilities.

### Treatment Recommendation

State the recommended intervention, plan, monitoring, referral, or no-treatment decision.

### Uncertainty Statements

List explicit uncertainty, conditionality, probability, caveats, or need for further evidence.

### Rationale

Describe the reasons, mechanisms, evidence, or clinical logic supporting the assessment and plan.

### Follow-up Plan

State next steps, monitoring, return criteria, additional tests, handoff instructions, or patient-facing actions.
