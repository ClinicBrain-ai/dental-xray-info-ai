# Dataset v0 Roadmap

This roadmap stages dataset growth from a proof-of-concept set to a stronger empirical pilot.

## Dataset v0.1: 10 Pairs

Purpose: proof of concept.

Composition: primarily synthetic or educational referral-consult pairs.

Goals:

- test the artifact schema;
- apply the reconstruction protocol;
- identify confusing coding categories;
- produce preliminary examples of preservation, drift, missing cognition, and new cognition.

Expected output: short feasibility memo and revised coding notes.

## Dataset v0.2: 25 Pairs

Purpose: feasibility pilot.

Composition: 10 synthetic pairs plus 15 published educational or public case-derived pairs where possible.

Goals:

- test coder consistency;
- refine scoring rules;
- compare synthetic and public educational cases;
- identify common artifact limitations.

Expected output: descriptive summary of transformation patterns.

## Dataset v0.3: 50 Pairs

Purpose: exploratory dataset.

Composition: mix of synthetic, public educational, and public case-derived pairs, ideally across two or three clinical domains.

Goals:

- compare transformation categories across domains;
- identify which artifact fields most improve reconstruction quality;
- refine the future real-world study protocol.

Expected output: draftable methods paper supplement or preprint-ready pilot report.

## Dataset v1.0: 100 Pairs

Purpose: stronger empirical pilot.

Composition: public and educational pairs, plus real-world de-identified records if governance permits.

Goals:

- estimate frequency of preservation, uncertainty loss, reframing, and new cognition within a defined domain;
- support inter-rater reliability analysis;
- prepare for real workflow artifact studies.

Expected output: publishable pilot study if dataset quality is sufficient.

## Growth Principle

Do not scale the dataset until the coding framework is stable enough that reviewers can explain disagreements clearly.
